UNESCO Background

UNESCO (United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization) seeks to build peace through international cooperation in Education, the Sciences, and Culture.

UNESCO was created to decide what kind of society we wanted to build together after the destruction of two World Wars. As early as 1942, global leaders began imagining an organization that would use education, culture, science and information to build lasting peace. Over the years, UNESCO has implemented initiatives that have radically improved lives around the world, and shaped global action in its field of competence, through nation-wide educational and scientific programmes, the creation of tsunami warning systems, and the idea of World Heritage. Today, we are continuing this work.
